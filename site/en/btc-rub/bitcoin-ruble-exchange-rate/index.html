BTC/RUB: bitcoin in Russian rubles

For Russian-speaking users BTC/RUB is the most relevant rate. We compute it as a cross: Binance BTC/USDT × MOEX USD/RUB (USD000UTSTOM, board CETS).

Alternatives are Bybit P2P order book or BestChange aggregators, but spreads there include a cash premium. The exchange cross is the cleanest objective rate.
